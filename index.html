Zining Wang

Welcome to my website! I am an Assistant Professor of Marketing at Carroll School of Management, Boston College. As an empirical modeler, I aim to utilize both econometric and deep learning methods to address marketing challenges. My primary research focuses on digital marketing, prosocial behavior, and automated video analysis.

I hold a Ph.D. from the University of British Columbia, an M.Sc. from Erasmus University, and a BEcon. from Beijing Foreign Studies University.
